Approach

I have worked as a counsellor/therapist for 25 years. In this time I have worked with a wide variety of clients in a large number of different settings. I work in an integrative way, using a personal centred foundation and also using other ideas from gestalt, solution focussed, mindfulness and art psychotherapy. I am interested in working with all sorts of issues including, relationships, bereavement, health issues, parenting, sexual abuse, domestic abuse, trauma (both past and present), workplace issues and life transitions. I recognise that people may have varied and unique combinations of difficulties and resources.
